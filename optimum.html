Frequently Asked Questions

What Internet plans does Optimum offer?
Optimum offers a variety of Internet plans to suit different needs, including high-speed options for households with multiple devices and heavy Internet usage.
How do I check if Optimum Internet is available in my area?
You can check the availability of Optimum Internet in your area by simply visiting the Optimum website and entering your address or by contacting Optimum customer service.
What speeds are available with Optimum Internet?
Optimum provides a range of Internet speeds from 300 Mbps up to 8 Gbps*. Optimum Internet plans offer speeds suitable for light browsing and streaming to ultra-fast gigabit options for intensive online activities and smart homes. Check what Internet speeds are available in your area.
How can I upgrade my current Optimum Internet plan?
To upgrade your current Optimum Internet plan, you can log into your Optimum account online, visit an Optimum store, or contact customer service for assistance.
Does Optimum offer any bundles with Internet service?
Yes, Optimum offers bundles that combine Internet service with TV, mobile, and home phone services, allowing you to save money while enjoying comprehensive home connectivity.
What equipment do I need for Optimum Internet?
For Optimum Internet, Optimum will provide you with a free compatible Gateway modem and a free WiFi extender, if needed. You can also use your own approved devices; however, Optimum cannot guarantee the reliability of the device.
How can I set up my Optimum Internet service?
Setting up your Optimum Internet service is easy. Once you receive your equipment, follow the Self-setup guide provided by Optimum or schedule a professional installation.
What is Optimum's customer support like?
Optimum offers 24/7 customer support via phone, online chat, and social media. Optimum also provides an extensive online help center with articles and troubleshooting guides.
Are there any data caps with Optimum Internet plans?
Optimum Internet plans come with Unlimited data, so you can browse, stream, and download without worrying about data caps or overage fees.
